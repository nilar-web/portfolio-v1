Classic Margherita Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 packet of dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil Leaves

Steps

  1. Mix yeast and warm water; let it sit for 5 minutes.
  2. Add flour and knead into a dough; let it rise for 1 hour.
  3. Roll out the dough and spread tomato sauce on top.
  4. Add mozzarella cheese and bake at 200°C (400°F) for 15 minutes.
  5. Garnish with fresh basil and serve hot.

Nutrition Facts

Nutrient Amount per Serving
Calories 250 kcal
Protein 10 g
Fat 8 g

Enjoy your homemade Margherita Pizza!
